About Us

Established in 2024, Poortjie Eatery is a fast-food outlet that not only serves up delicious, quick meals but also provides a seamless delivery service for local fast food restaurants. Based in the heart of the community, our mission is to make it easier for customers to enjoy their favorite meals without leaving the comfort of their homes. Whether you're craving something from our own menu or a dish from another local spot, Poortjie Eatery is here to satisfy your hunger with speed and convenience. We are committed to quality service, ensuring every delivery is fresh and on time.
